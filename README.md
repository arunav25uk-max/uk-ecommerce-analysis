# UK E-Commerce Customer & Revenue Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of 500,000+ retail transactions from a UK-based online retailer 
to identify high-value customer segments and deliver actionable retention 
recommendations using RFM segmentation.

## Business Question
Which customers drive the most revenue, and what should the business do 
to protect and grow that revenue?

## Key Findings
- Analysed 397,884 clean transactions across 4,338 unique customers
- Top 5% of customers (216 individuals) generate 50.3% of total revenue
- Identified 5 behavioural segments: Champions, Loyal, Recent, At Risk, Lost
- Revenue shows strong seasonality peaking in November 2011

## Recommendations
1. Protect Champions with a tiered loyalty programme
2. Re-engage At-Risk high-value customers with targeted campaigns
3. Upsell Loyal customers to Champion tier via personalised recommendations
